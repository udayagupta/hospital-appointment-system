# How will the app work?

## For patient

- First - register or login if exists
- look for thier doctor

- check if the doctor has a empty slot on patient's preferred date and time
- if there is an empty slot, book the appointment
- if there is no empty slot, ask the patient to choose another date and time

- after booking the appointment, the patient can view and manage their appointments in the patient portal.
- after the appointment the patient will receive a notification.

## For doctor

- First register or login if exists
- view and manage appointments in the doctor portal, on a calender
- check the patient, update the patient slots, and update the follow-up patients
